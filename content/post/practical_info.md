## Dates

The courses will take place at the same time, in the two days leading up to
[ESCAIDE](https://www.escaide.eu/en), on Monday 25 and Tuesday 26th
November 2019.

* **Monday 25 November**
    + **9:00-9:30** Registrations
    + **9:30** Courses start
    + **17:00** Courses end

* **Tuesday 26 November**
    + **9:30** Courses start
    + **17:00** Courses end
